Trauma & PTSD

Childhood trauma, abuse, workplace wounds, and relational pain often leave patterns that feel hard to break. We go beyond managing symptoms and coping strategies to address what’s underneath, so you can feel safe in your body, clear in your mind, and free to move forward without being defined by what happened to you.

Narcissistic abuse recovery

Growing up around narcissistic behavior, or repeatedly finding yourself in those dynamics, can silence your voice, distort your reality, and disconnect you from your worth. I understand this cycle personally. Here, we help you see the pattern clearly, break its hold, and reclaim your voice so you can rebuild identity, boundaries, and self-trust.
