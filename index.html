What we transform together

You may be carrying
more than one of these.

Whatever you’re carrying right now, you’re not alone in it. Whether you’re in survival mode or standing at the edge of your purpose, feeling stuck and unsure how to move forward, this is where things begin to shift. Survival doesn’t get the final word. You do.

Trauma & PTSD

Childhood trauma, abuse, workplace wounds, and relational pain often leave patterns that feel hard to break. We go beyond managing symptoms and coping strategies to address what’s underneath, so you can feel safe in your body, clear in your mind, and free to move forward without being defined by what happened to you.

Narcissistic abuse recovery

Growing up around narcissistic behavior, or repeatedly finding yourself in those dynamics, can silence your voice, distort your reality, and disconnect you from your worth. I understand this cycle personally. Here, we help you see the pattern clearly, break its hold, and reclaim your voice so you can rebuild identity, boundaries, and self-trust.

Identity & inner healing

Rediscover who you are beneath the conditioning, expectations, and experiences that shaped you. This work helps you reconnect with your true identity, rebuild self-trust, and step out of patterns that no longer reflect who you’re becoming.

Purpose & calling

Being multi-gifted can feel like a gift and a burden at the same time—too many directions, not enough clarity. We help you cut through the noise, identify your core lane, and build a clear, actionable roadmap so you can stop circling your potential and start building what you were actually designed for.

Mindset & self-sabotage

Most self-sabotage isn’t random, it’s learned at the subconscious level. Through intentional inner work, I help you uncover and interrupt these patterns, release limiting beliefs, and rewire the thought systems that have kept you stuck in repetition. This is a grounded, strategic process of replacing internalized lies with truth that supports lasting change.

Leadership & Business Clarity

I work with entrepreneurs, leaders, and early-stage founders to bridge the gap between vision and execution. With experience building businesses myself and supporting startups through SBDC advisory work, I understand both the strategic and internal challenges that come with growth. This work focuses on clearing mindset blocks, organizing scattered ideas, and creating grounded decision-making so you can build with clarity, confidence, and sustainability.
